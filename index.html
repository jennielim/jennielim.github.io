Introduction

Hi, I'm Jennie! I'm a junior, graduating May '24, at the University of Minnesota, Twin Cities majoring in Computer Science.

My experiences include front end development as a Student Web Developer at UMN and full stack development as a Software Engineer Intern at Allstate Insurance. Additionally, I work as a TA at UMN, assisting students with programming concepts in Python. I am also the Secretary of ACM-W here at the university. I am proficient in Python, Java, and C, and I have experience in OCaml, Assembly, React, CSS, and HTML .

My skills are well rounded; and I am quick, motivated, excited, and determined to learn more. I enjoy collaborating with others and solving problems.

Take a look at my resume, CV - a more detailed version of my experiences, projects, and recognition. I hope you get a good idea of who I am by browsing through the site. Thanks for stopping by!
